What Are Cookies And How Does This Website Use Them?

Cookies are small pieces of data stored in text files that are saved on your computer or other devices when websites are loaded in a browser. DGApps.ca uses them to remember your dark mode preferences (i.e. should the website be displayed via dark or light mode) and for the cookie policy popup for a single visit (through a "session cookie").

Session cookies are temporary cookies that are used during the course of your visit to the Website, and they expire when you close the web browser.

All the cookies used are temporary and remain on your local computer only for the duration while you are on the website. No data is shared with any 3rd parties and all data is erased when you close your browser.

If you don't like the idea of cookies or certain types of cookies, you can change your browser's settings to delete cookies that have already been set and to not accept new cookies. To learn more about how to do this or to learn more about cookies, visit internetcookies.org

Or you can avoid using the dark mode toggle located in the bottom right hand corner of the menu.
